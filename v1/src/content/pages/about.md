## Company Overview

DES Engineering Services and Manpower Supply Limited is a Hong Kong-based company providing professional engineering services and skilled manpower for electrical, control, and instrumentation (EC&I) projects.

We support contractors, project managers, and industrial clients with reliable technical services that keep projects on track.

## Mission

To deliver dependable engineering services and skilled manpower that meet the highest standards of safety, quality, and professionalism.

## Vision

To be the trusted EC&I services partner of choice for industrial and commercial projects across the region.

## Core Values

- **Professionalism**: We conduct all work with technical excellence and integrity
- **Reliability**: We deliver on our commitments, on time and within scope
- **Safety**: We protect our team, our clients, and our reputation through safe work practices
- **Quality**: We maintain high standards in every task we perform

## Why Clients Choose DES

- **Experienced Team**: Our engineers and technicians bring deep industry knowledge
- **Flexible Manpower**: We provide skilled resources that match your project scale
- **End-to-End Support**: From installation to commissioning to maintenance
- **Local Presence**: We understand the Hong Kong market and project environment

## Safety and Quality Commitment

DES maintains strict safety protocols and quality control procedures on all projects. Our team follows recognized industry standards and site-specific requirements to ensure work is performed correctly and safely.

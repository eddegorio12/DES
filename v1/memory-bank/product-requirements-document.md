# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## DES Website Improvement
**Version:** 1.0  
**Company:** DES Engineering Services and Manpower Supply Limited  
**Document Owner:** DES  
**Product Type:** Marketing website / lead-generation website  
**Status:** Draft  

---

## 1. Overview

DES has an existing company website that communicates its engineering services and manpower supply offerings. The current site establishes the company’s presence, but it can be improved to present DES more clearly, build more trust with potential clients, and generate more qualified inquiries.

This project will redesign and improve the website to make it more modern, more professional, more conversion-focused, and more SEO-friendly.

The new website should help DES:
- explain what the company does within the first few seconds
- present services in a more structured and credible way
- improve trust through clearer company information and proof points
- increase contact form submissions, calls, and WhatsApp inquiries
- rank better for relevant service-related searches

---

## 2. Problem Statement

The current site provides basic information, but it has several limitations:

- the value proposition is broad and not immediately clear
- the messaging is generic and not strongly differentiated
- service descriptions are brief and not organized for buyer decision-making
- the site does not strongly highlight trust signals such as company credentials, project capability, process, safety, or experience
- the site structure is simple but not optimized for conversion
- SEO foundations appear limited, making it harder for the site to attract organic traffic
- the contact experience can be strengthened with clearer calls to action and lead capture

Because of this, potential clients may not quickly understand:
- what DES specializes in
- why they should trust DES
- which exact services are available
- how to take the next step

---

## 3. Product Goal

Create a professional, conversion-focused company website that positions DES as a credible engineering services and manpower supply partner for EC&I-related work and related field services.

## 10. Product Requirements

## 10.1 Homepage Requirements

The homepage must:
- clearly state what DES does in the hero section
- include a strong headline and subheadline
- include a primary CTA such as “Request a Quote” or “Talk to Us”
- include a secondary CTA such as WhatsApp or Call Now
- show key services at a glance
- include trust signals such as:
  - registered company status
  - years of experience
  - industries served
  - safety or quality commitment
  - project readiness
- include a short “Why DES” section
- include a service overview with links to detail pages
- include contact options above the footer
- be optimized for mobile first

### Recommended Homepage Messaging Direction
**Headline example:**  
Reliable EC&I Engineering Services and Manpower Supply in Hong Kong

**Subheadline example:**  
DES supports industrial and commercial projects with dependable electrical, control, instrumentation, installation, commissioning, inspection, and maintenance services.

---

## 10.2 About Page Requirements

The About page must:
- explain who DES is
- clarify company background and positioning
- include mission, vision, and company values
- highlight professionalism, safety, and reliability
- explain the company’s expertise in EC&I and manpower supply
- include a stronger trust narrative, not just short mission/vision text
- include a CTA to contact DES

### Recommended About Content Blocks
- Company Overview
- Mission
- Vision
- Core Values
- Why Clients Choose DES
- Commitment to Safety and Quality
- Contact CTA

---

## 10.3 Services Page Requirements

The Services section must:
- organize services clearly
- present each service with business value, not only technical wording
- explain what each service includes
- explain who the service is for
- include proof points, benefits, or process
- include CTA per service page

### Required Service Content Format
Each service page should include:
- service title
- short summary
- what the service covers
- common client needs/problems solved
- why DES is qualified
- CTA section

### Example Service Page Structure
1. Hero section
2. Service overview
3. Scope of work
4. Benefits to client
5. Process or delivery approach
6. Industries/use cases
7. Contact CTA

---

## 10.4 Contact Page Requirements

The Contact page must:
- make it easy to contact DES quickly
- include a short lead form
- clearly show phone number
- clearly show email address

- clearly show WhatsApp CTA

### Contact CTA Examples

- keyword-focused service pages
- internal linking between pages
- optimized image alt text
- clean URLs
- mobile-friendly design
- fast page speed
- schema markup where relevant
- indexable page structure

### Suggested Keyword Themes

- instrumentation services Hong Kong
- cable tray installation Hong Kong
- cable pulling services Hong Kong
- loop check pre-commissioning Hong Kong
- calibration services Hong Kong
- electrical maintenance Hong Kong
- manpower supply Hong Kong engineering
- industrial electrical services Hong Kong

### Content SEO Recommendations
- create one optimized page per major service
- include location relevance naturally
- use plain-language explanations alongside technical terms
- add FAQ sections on service pages where useful
